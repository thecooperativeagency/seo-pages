What Makes the 3 Series Different

50/50 Weight Distribution
Most cars are nose-heavy. The 3 Series balances its weight perfectly between front and rear, giving you cornering neutrality no front-drive sedan can match. The car pivots around you — not away from you.
Rear-Wheel Drive
The 330i is RWD by default. It's how BMWs are meant to be driven — power going to the rear, front wheels steering, physics working for you. The result is a natural, balanced driving experience no front-drive car can replicate.
Communicative Steering
You don't just point the 3 Series, you talk to it. Road feel flows through the wheel so you always know exactly what the tires are doing. That feedback is what separates driving from merely traveling.
M Sport Suspension
Sport-tuned from the factory. Firm without being punishing. Body roll is managed without sacrificing ride quality on Louisiana roads — no small feat given what we put cars through down here.
