BMW's Flagship SAV

The X7 is BMW's largest, most luxurious SAV. Three rows. Up to 7 passengers. Available 4.4L V8 with 523 horsepower. Yet it still drives like a BMW — responsive, confident, more agile than its size suggests. This is what happens when BMW engineers are given no constraints and told to build the best luxury 3-row vehicle in the world.

The results speak for themselves. The X7's long wheelbase creates a rear cabin that rivals dedicated limousines in legroom and refinement. Standard air suspension absorbs imperfections with total composure. The massive kidney grille — controversial when it debuted, now widely imitated — announces your arrival in a way nothing else in this segment does. Available captain's chairs in the second row turn the X7 into a 6-seat private lounge on wheels.

If you need to move the whole family without compromising on anything — the drive, the interior, the technology, the presence — the X7 is the only answer BMW needed to build.
