Technology & Interior: Driver-First Design

The X1's BMW Curved Display is a 10.25-inch information display paired with a 10.7-inch central touchscreen, running BMW's latest iDrive system with natural voice control, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and over-the-air updates. The cockpit is oriented around the driver — every control falls to hand, every screen angle is intentional.

The GLB uses Mercedes' MBUX system with dual 10.25-inch screens. It's competent. But BMW's iDrive is faster to respond, more intuitive to navigate while driving, and integrates more deeply with your phone. When you're navigating Baton Rouge traffic on Bluebonnet or Florida Boulevard, the difference in interface speed matters more than you'd expect.
