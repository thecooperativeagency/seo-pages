When the Sensors Don't Match the Repair, the Safety Story Breaks.

Lane keeping, adaptive cruise, parking sensors, automatic braking, blind-spot monitoring — modern BMW driver-assistance features depend on cameras and sensors aimed within tight tolerances. After a collision, those tolerances can move even when the bodywork looks straight.

  • Calibration is part of the certified process — not an optional add-on after the panels are bolted on.
  • BMW-defined procedures guide how sensors are aimed and verified following structural or glass work.
  • Glass replacement is coordinated with the calibrations and seals modern BMWs depend on.
